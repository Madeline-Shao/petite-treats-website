About Us

Petite Treats is a home-based bakery in Cupertino/West San Jose. We make specialty macarons and other baked goods, all of which are personally made from scratch with love and care every day. In this time of uncertainty, we want to add a little love and joy to our customers' lives with our delicious handmade treats. :)
